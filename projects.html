Chaos Crashers | Nov 15, 2025 – Current


Chaos Crashers is a indie rogue like hack and slash written in GO for the ebitengine. It is co-developed with my friend Kio Ippolito who produces assets and helps game design decisions.

Features:

•Object oriented enemy ai

•toggle-able camera tracking

•Robust procedural dungeon generation

•Controller support with omni-directional inputs

•Playable and navigateable dungeon with room permanence
